# Types of faxes ## Laboratory results These results are usually sent by MMHH, LabCorp or Quest. They will show wheter "Positive" or "Negative", or different ranges to determine exam values. - Save in: "Lab documents" folder - Document name: (Name of facility) Labs results (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assign to: Lab department ## Imaging results This refers to X-rays, USG, CT-scans, and other imaging reports. These are typically faxes sent by MMHH, Solis, SimonMed, HCA, Houston MRI and other imaging centers. - Save in: "X-ray documents" folder - Document name: (Exam name) (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assign to: Lab department > Make sure to only upload DI to patient's record, due to somnetimes DI of different pt's come together (in the same file). ## Prior authorization These documents usually come from the insurance, and serve to apply for imaging, physical therapy, and other procedures that need to be authorized by insurance. PA's have statuses, which are "approved", "denied", or "in progress". - If the PA is approved there should be an encounter. - If it's a PA follow up disregard, unless there's no PA document in chart. - Save in: "ePA" folder - Document name: PA (status) (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assign to: PA department ## Covermymeds Like PA but for medications. These are NOT REFILLS, but rather focus on the approval process itself [^1]. - Save in "ePA" folder - Document name: Covermymeds (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assign to: PA department > Actually there's no need to assign it to PA department and leave it open, we should reviewed, due to the majority of the cases they received the PA via Covermymeds [^1]: We will continue to receive follow ups until it's done. If PA department already has document w/key assigned, disregard fax. **Check that key matches before disregarding** ## Prescriptions refill/Change/Denied/Rejection From: Walgreens, HEB, CVS, Walmart, etc. Usually 90-day refill covermymeds in which a refill is being requested or denied. We should check if fulfilled if confirmed, upload & review. - Save in: "Chart documents" folder - Document name: Prescription refill (MM.DD.YYYY) - Asign to: Corresponding MA ## Progress Note/OT/PT/Urgent care You will see typical progress notes from different offices (MMHH, Cardiovascular, Physical therapy, etc.) that are usually labeled as "revied & save". It wont be necessary to assign this kind of documents to anyone unless they are required, or unles they contain labs or imaging reports, in which case they're handled as such. If it's a PN as part of MR you should assign to MR. **U should specify if it's a full record.** - Save in: "Chart documents" folder - Document name: - (Name of facility)-PN-(MM.DD.YYYY) - (Facility name) ~~Full records~~ received records - MM.DD.YYYY (recibido) - Assign to: Nobody > If the PN released is from ER visit or SX: ***U must create an encounter.*** ## Medical records Certain insurance companies, brokers, and medical provider may request documentation related to appt and medical results, typically for continuation of care, payment auditing purposes, or to support a patient's elegibility for life insurance benefits. - Save in: "Patient documents" folder - Document name: MR request (Name of facility) (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assign to: Medical records department ## Notifications These notifications can come from insurance itself, from covermymeds or even from the othe health facilities. Some types of notifications also are: 1. Notice of no answer 2. Notice of admission 3. Notice of discharge 4. Notices of immunization[^2] 5. Resumes (RN, hiring)[^3] - Appt: there's no need to asign to anybody, just review it. - Notification of referrals should be assign to the corresponding MA - Pick up RX and soliciting/Ads should be *jeter* - Save in Chart documents folder. [^2]: This notif. should be assign to corresponding MA [^3]: This archives should be saved in Drive. ## Signatures Sometimes certain org will request signatures & statments for social disability benefits or other types of assistance. **MA has obligation to follow up after assigned.** Sign plan of care, clearance, disability benefits: - Save in "Patients documents" - Document name: (Type of doc) signature required (MM.DD.YYYY) - Assing to: corresponding MA ## Referrals - Document name: (Name of facility/provider) referral (status) (MM.DD.YYYY) - Save in: Referral notes - Assign to: - If denied: PA department - If approved: Review - If req: PA department
